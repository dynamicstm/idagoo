iDagoo

Integrated Crowdsourced Content Signaling, Sourcing, Workflow Management and Multiplatform Distribution PaaS.

It aims to unify content sourcing, editorial workflow Management and publishing across multiplatform in a seamless manner from local context by freelancers (contributors) that authenticate stories on the ground by any local or global media with incentivisation models that are beneficial in a true win-win fashion.

Distributed peer reporters at the local level source stories or cover happenings and make it available at the story signaling dashboard for any subscribed nationwide, regional or international media house. The mainstream media outlets as subscriber would have a toolset where they can check for emerging signals and engage with the local original content creator. Once an agreement is reached between the two sides they either start developing the story in a unified manner in iDagoo or use part of the sourced information in their stories as an anecdote. Or the original freelancer would go and publish it under his own brand.
